About Me

Hello! I'm Billy Moulton, an experienced yoga teacher from Ireland, passionate about guiding others in their yoga journey. With certifications in Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin Yoga, and Thai massage, I've taught in beautiful locations like Thailand and Sri Lanka. My classes are dynamic, tailored to student needs, and infused with creativity and kindness. Join me on my online classes to explore the transformative power of yoga together. Namaste!

Qualifications

I am a highly qualified yoga teacher with 200 and 300-hour yoga teacher training certifications, along with 60 hours of Thai massage training. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Business and Entrepreneurship.

Skills

I am creative, passionate, organized, communicative, calm, and observant. These skills help me connect deeply with students and create a supportive, enriching yoga experience.

Expertise

I specialize in Hatha, Yin, and Vinyasa yoga, offering both slow and power flow sessions. I am skilled in breathwork and pranayama, as well as massage techniques.
