Yin Yoga

Yin yoga is a mindful, slow-paced, meditative style of yoga. This style involves long, deep holding of passive seated and lying poses.

Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa yoga is a fast-paced, challenging yoga practice. It's a more athletic style of yoga and involves moving from one pose directly into the next while maintaining connection with the breath.

Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga style links physical poses with controlled breathing, and focuses on moving your body slowly and deliberately into different poses that build strength and flexibility.
